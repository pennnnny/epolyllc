PK/PD MODELING SERVICE

Pharmacometrics is a fresh field in clinical research that combines data on drugs, diseases, patients, and trials. It analyzes this information to create models, aiding in improved drug development and regulatory decisions. These models showcase how drug exposure relates to the body's response, considering both desired and undesired effects, along with individual patient traits.
